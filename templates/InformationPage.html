Our Purpose

The data we collect on student progress is crucial for the continuous improvement of our academic programs and student services. By understanding your achievements and experiences, we can enhance the overall quality of education and support at Glasgow International College.

Benefits of Data Collection

Contributing to our data collection helps us to identify areas of success and opportunities for improvement. It ensures that your academic journey and experience at GIC are fulfilling and enriching.
